Welcome to the UMBC Indian Student Community Hub!

Your all-in-one guide. Our mission is to simplify your transition, offering a helping hand and a friendly face as you embark on this life-changing journey. With resources for finding affordable housing around campus, academic assistance of all forms, cultural experiences, and vibrant connections, we’re here to ensure you thrive in your new academic home.

Our team is comprised of current and former UMBC students who understand the challenges and opportunities you would face. So, dive in, explore what we have to offer, and let’s make your time at UMBC an unforgettable one!
